THE START OF SOMETHING NEW

Our fellow habitants of the planet Earth, may we ask you a few questions:

Ø  Is everything going right on our planet?

Ø  Is our way of living is perfect?

Ø  Should nothing be changed in our environment and ourselves?

If you didn’t answer “Yes” to these questions, this site is for you.

The next and the main question: How could we get out from all this?

The recipe is simple:

ü  Reveal and formulate the principal problems those the mankind has now.

ü  Look for all the possible solutions for the each problem.

ü  Choose the optimal solution or their optimal combination in each case.

ü  Try to solve each problem using chosen solutions.

We follow this recipe in our everyday life using our mind. But each our mind, even a genius one, is too weak to follow it in a life of all the mankind. Let’s combine abilities of our brains (modest or genius) into one decision-making conscious. Global problems need

 

The Global Mind

And it is here!

Our hearts beat separately – let our minds work together!
